# MARINE INTELLIGENCE TAXONOMY

## Waterway Intelligence

Purpose:
Understand river conditions and navigability.

Inputs:
- Water depth
- Flow stability
- Water level variation

Outputs:
- Navigation suitability
- Waterway risk
- Route recommendations

Use Cases:
- Vessel routing
- Draft planning

---
