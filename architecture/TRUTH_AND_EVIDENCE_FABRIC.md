# TRUTH AND EVIDENCE FABRIC

## Purpose

The Truth and Evidence Fabric is the foundation of trust inside the Marine Intelligence System.

Its purpose is to ensure that no recommendation, risk assessment, opportunity assessment or operational decision can exist without complete supporting evidence.

Every output generated by NICAI must be:

* Explainable
* Traceable
* Replayable
* Verifiable
* Governed

The fabric creates a permanent chain between observations, evidence, reasoning and decisions.

---

# Core Principle

Nothing is accepted as truth simply because a system says it is true.

Every intelligence output must prove:

What was observed?

↓

What evidence exists?

↓

What relationships exist?

↓

What reasoning occurred?

↓

What decision was produced?

↓

Who validated it?

↓

Why should it be trusted?
